TL;DR This is my site, I have a say about what's on it, yes even the comments.

Having said that you will find I'm pretty reasonable. If you and I don't agree on what reasonable
means, see above. The Internet is a big place maybe your views will be welcome somewhere else. Or
you could create your own site and say what you want there. Please also remember that this is
a technology, open source programming in C++ site. Your comments should fit into those topics. If
they don't they are going to have to be particularly insightful, interesting or informative to
stay. In fact they probably deserve their own site, but they would be allowed to bunk here until
they have a permanent home.
